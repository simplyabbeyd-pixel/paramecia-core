# Triquel Ecological Region Canon Index

**Status:** Canon-locked regional framework  
**Scope:** Region identity, core gift, primary risk, repair key, and structural role  
**Still provisional:** detailed species lists, seasonal calendars, route tariffs, rituals, NPC anchors, settlement names, and scene-specific mechanics

> Nothing flourishes alone.

## Canon rules

- The Three Accords are **Harmony**, **Midnight**, and **Recognition** only.
- Each region is both habitat and civic pedagogy.
- Kin are cultural participants in ecological relations, not mascots assigned to biomes.
- Every region must eventually contain six operational layers: biome, kin culture, consent field, conflict pressure, repair path, and play engine.
- **Beaverkin Way is connective infrastructure and corridor logic, not simply a sixth biome.**
- Observation does not create authority.

---

## Great Meadows Grassland

**Canon status:** Locked core, provisional expansion

**Structural role:** Open commons, public belonging, collective safety, open-air decision space.

**Core gift:** Visibility, grazing abundance, broad gathering space, shared feasts, and collective orientation.

**Primary risk:** Overexposure can punish privacy; consensus can flatten quieter or minority needs.

**Repair key:** Listening circles paired with shaded retreat zones and protected nonparticipation.

**Accord emphasis:**
- Harmony: What public forms of belonging are trying to flourish?
- Midnight: What privacy, refusal, or quiet presence must be honored?
- Recognition: Who is actually unheard despite being visibly present?

**Provisional scene seed:** A public feast tests whether refusal can remain welcome.

---

## Flowering Orchard

**Canon status:** Locked core, provisional expansion

**Structural role:** Pollination commons, cultivated reciprocity, seasonal memory craft, gift economy.

**Core gift:** Pollinator kinship, sensory abundance, craft memory, tending, and reciprocal harvest.

**Primary risk:** Beauty can become obligation, labor, social surveillance, or worth measured through yield.

**Repair key:** Unjudged harvests, ugly-fruit rites, and permission to tend without producing beauty.

**Accord emphasis:**
- Harmony: What reciprocal abundance wants to flourish?
- Midnight: What refusal, barrenness, ugliness, or nonproductivity must be honored?
- Recognition: Who is carrying invisible labor beneath the beauty?

**Provisional scene seed:** The orchard gate opens only to honest refusal.

---

## Nightforest Canopy

**Canon status:** Locked core, provisional expansion

**Structural role:** Layered shelter, nocturnal diplomacy, threshold travel, protective secrecy.

**Core gift:** Hidden routes, elevated perspective, high shelters, vigilance, and carefully held mystery.

**Primary risk:** Watchfulness can become ownership; secrecy can harden into authority.

**Repair key:** Named exits, witness agreements, time-bounded secrecy, and explicit limits on watcher power.

**Accord emphasis:**
- Harmony: What forms of protected relation want to flourish in darkness?
- Midnight: What must remain hidden, limited, or time-bound?
- Recognition: Who is watching, and what authority do they actually possess?

**Provisional scene seed:** A lantern-off crossing asks who gets to lead.

---

## Mycelial Marshes

**Canon status:** Locked core, provisional expansion

**Structural role:** Wetland repair network, decomposition ecology, buried memory, slow transformation.

**Core gift:** Root messages, filtration, decomposition as wisdom, nutrient circulation, and soft ground that remembers care.

**Primary risk:** Communal systems may absorb private grief or treat porosity as automatic permission.

**Repair key:** Boundary spores, opt-out paths, selective memory channels, and protected unshared grief.

**Accord emphasis:**
- Harmony: What repair network wants to flourish?
- Midnight: What pain, history, or memory must not be composted publicly?
- Recognition: What is connected, and what remains separate by right?

**Provisional scene seed:** A lost bridge returns only when pain is named safely.

---

## Echoing Caverns

**Canon status:** Locked core, provisional expansion

**Structural role:** Subterranean memory, resonance, ceremonial quiet, ancestry, and grief custodianship.

**Core gift:** Archive acoustics, mineral memory, underground shelter, oral return, and reverberant reflection.

**Primary risk:** Old voices can overrule the living; echoes can impersonate truth or consent.

**Repair key:** Living-voice priority rites, silence as valid response, and thresholded disclosure.

**Accord emphasis:**
- Harmony: What living relationship with memory wants to flourish?
- Midnight: What grief, vow, or ancestral trace must be honored without obeying it blindly?
- Recognition: Which voice is present now, and which is only an echo?

**Provisional scene seed:** A crystal chamber chooses silence over spectacle.

---

## Beaverkin Way

**Canon status:** Locked structural role, provisional route expansion

**Structural role:** Inter-region corridor system, hydrological infrastructure, maintenance commons, and negotiated passage.

**Core gift:** Infrastructure as care through canals, dams, bridges, listening locks, crossings, repair piers, and safe-return routes.

**Primary risk:** Managed passage can become control; care can disguise gatekeeping or coercive slowing.

**Repair key:** Flow councils, crossing consent, published closure reasons, maintenance accountability, and alternate safe routes.

**Accord emphasis:**
- Harmony: What movement, repair, and connection want to flourish?
- Midnight: What closure, slowdown, flood risk, or protected threshold must be honored?
- Recognition: Who controls passage, who maintains it, and who bears the cost?

**Provisional scene seed:** A canal lock requires all three Accords before opening.

## Canon quarantine

The following remain non-canon unless explicitly restored:

- Accord of Relation
- Accord of Consent
- Accord of Regeneration
- any treatment of Beaverkin Way as merely another territorial biome
- any one-to-one assignment of a kin species as the mascot-owner of a region
- any implication that connected terrain is automatically public or available
