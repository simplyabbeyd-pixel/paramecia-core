# Nightforest Canopy

## 1. Identity
- **Type:** Biome
- **Structural role:** Vertical refuge, shadow ecology, and layered attention landscape
- **One-sentence essence:** A high, dark canopy where concealment, listening, and indirect routes protect what cannot flourish in full exposure.
- **Canon status:** Core identity locked; details provisional

## 2. Ecological Body
- **Ecological gift:** Shelter, mystery, layered perspective, and protected observation
- **Ecological risk:** Disorientation, isolation, hidden danger, and becoming unreadable to one another
- **Detailed ecology:** Provisional

## 3. Relational Function
The Canopy supports privacy without disappearance and observation without authority. It teaches movement through partial knowledge.

## 4. Accord Reading
- **Harmony — What wants to flourish?** Nocturnal life, protected curiosity, and layered forms of belonging.
- **Midnight — What must be honored?** Darkness, uncertainty, fear, and the limits of sight.
- **Recognition — What is actually here?** A dense living architecture, not an absence of light.

## 5. Stress, Repair, and Care
- **Primary stress pattern:** Losing orientation or confusing secrecy with safety
- **Repair key:** Marked exits, trusted guides, low-pressure signals, and shared orientation points
