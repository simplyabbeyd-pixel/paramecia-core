# Echoing Caverns

## 1. Identity
- **Type:** Biome
- **Structural role:** Resonance, memory, and deep-listening landscape
- **One-sentence essence:** A cavern system where voices, silences, and histories return altered but not erased.
- **Canon status:** Core identity locked; details provisional

## 2. Ecological Body
- **Ecological gift:** Resonance, memory, acoustic orientation, and deep witness
- **Ecological risk:** Amplification, looping distress, distortion, and being trapped inside an old echo
- **Detailed ecology:** Provisional

## 3. Relational Function
The Caverns make consequences audible. They support remembrance and witness while requiring care around repetition, volume, and inherited voices.

## 4. Accord Reading
- **Harmony — What wants to flourish?** Honest resonance, remembered truth, and voices finding responsive space.
- **Midnight — What must be honored?** Silence, grief, distortion, and memories that cannot yet be resolved.
- **Recognition — What is actually here?** An active chamber of return, not a neutral container.

## 5. Stress, Repair, and Care
- **Primary stress pattern:** Echo loops that intensify rather than clarify
- **Repair key:** Dampening chambers, pauses, trusted witnesses, and deliberate changes of route or register

## 6. Atlas and Story Use
Map symbols, rituals, seasonal events, and kin anchors remain provisional for Living Atlas Part III.
