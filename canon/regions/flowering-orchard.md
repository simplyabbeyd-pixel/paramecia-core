# Flowering Orchard

## 1. Identity
- **Type:** Biome
- **Structural role:** Cultivated abundance and reciprocal tending landscape
- **One-sentence essence:** A flowering food-forest where nourishment depends on mutual care rather than extraction.
- **Canon status:** Core identity locked; details provisional

## 2. Ecological Body
- **Ecological gift:** Nourishment, sweetness, cultivation, and seasonal reciprocity
- **Ecological risk:** Overharvesting, obligation, and confusing abundance with infinite availability
- **Detailed ecology:** Provisional

## 3. Relational Function
The Orchard teaches that gifts have seasons, limits, and caretaking requirements. Receiving and tending belong to the same cycle.

## 4. Accord Reading
- **Harmony — What wants to flourish?** Nourishment, pollination, beauty, and shared harvest.
- **Midnight — What must be honored?** Scarcity, dormancy, labor, and the right of living systems not to yield.
- **Recognition — What is actually here?** A cultivated relationship, not a passive resource.

## 5. Stress, Repair, and Care
- **Primary stress pattern:** Taking without tending or turning care into debt
- **Repair key:** Seasonal limits, reciprocal labor, rest, and explicit permission before harvest

## 6. Atlas and Story Use
Map symbols, rituals, seasonal events, and kin anchors remain provisional for Living Atlas Part III.
