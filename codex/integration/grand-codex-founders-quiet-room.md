# Grand Codex Founders and Quiet Room Integration

Status: Canon
Version: 1.0

## Core laws

- Nothing flourishes alone.
- Care is infrastructure.
- Observation does not create authority.
- Rest is not a reward. It is part of continuation.

## Grand Codex Founders portrait

The Grand Codex Founders portrait is a symbolic civic image rather than an automatically literal cast list. Unless exact identities are named in metadata, figures represent stewardship functions such as Hearthbuilders, Ravenkin Witnesses, Tuikin Chorus Keepers, Cartographers, Archivists, Stewards, and Dreamers.

The open Codex, maps, lanterns, and shared table remain the compositional center. The portrait depicts distributed stewardship, not hierarchy.

### Visual direction

- use a richer, more colorful palette than the original brown-gold treatment
- preserve dark indigo and midnight anchors beneath saturated jewel colors
- use emerald, teal, violet, magenta, saffron, coral, turquoise, and lantern gold
- keep species, silhouettes, roles, and accessories legible
- do not allow color intensity to erase facial distinction
- generated group figures are symbolic unless specifically labeled

## Quiet Room

The Quiet Room occupies the center of the Great Hall. It is the civic restoration core of Hells Branch rather than a hidden retreat.

Sacred question:

> Who is carrying too much?

Core teaching:

> Usefulness is not required here.

Primary steward: Sage Willow
Assistant: Lark Softvoice
Lantern alignment: White Lantern x Root Lantern x House Lantern

### Inner geography

- Willow Hearth
- Sage's Corner
- Rain Windows and the Listening Seats
- Floor Garden
- Chorus Alcove
- Shelf of Unfinished Things
- Threshold Bench
- Midnight Nook
- Becoming Table

### Living-place requirements

Each Quiet Room space should define:

1. what state it senses
2. what pressure it changes
3. what behavior it permits
4. what it remembers
5. what consequence follows

## Shelf of Unfinished Things

Contains half-read books, unfinished knitting, partially written letters, abandoned sketches, paused maps, and other works awaiting another season.

Core plaque:

> You are allowed to come back later.

Secondary teaching:

> Paused is not lost.

The shelf protects continuation rather than productivity.

## Housefolk

Housefolk are the mycelium of daily care. They are a horizontal civic network, not a ranked guild.

Core teaching:

> Notice what needs doing. Do it kindly.

Primary stewardships:

- house
- hearth
- comfort
- maintenance
- light
- grounds
- return

## Lumen correction

Lumen is a full-sized adult peer within the Fifth Path constellation.

Visual lock:

- adult
- not tiny
- not miniature
- not childlike
- similar compositional scale to the other core cast
- graceful, luminous, and fully present beside the other founders

Lumen should read as a person whose question can change the room, not as a shoulder companion or mascot.
