# Emberfox Logo Specification

## Logo Name

**The Lantern Compass**

## Core Mark

A fox head held inside an incomplete circular lantern frame. Two feather-leaf forms flank the face, and a small four-point star rests above the brow. The open ring signals that becoming is never sealed.

```text
        ✦
    ╭───🏮───╮
  🌿    🦊    🌿
    ╲   ◇   ╱
      ╰─╴╶─╯
```

The text sketch is symbolic, not the final vector geometry.

## Symbol Components

- **Fox head:** curiosity, route-finding, adaptive intelligence
- **Lantern frame:** witness, orientation, care, welcome
- **Compass diamond:** location, relationship mapping, Point 0
- **Feather-leaves:** memory joined with ecological growth
- **Four-point star:** possibility made visible
- **Incomplete ring:** open canon, continued becoming, non-totalizing stewardship

## Logo Variants

### Primary Seal
Full circular mark with fox, lantern, compass, leaves, and star.

### Repository Avatar
Fox head, compass diamond, and small star only.

### Monochrome Mark
Single-color line mark for documentation, stamps, and terminal output.

### Tiny Favicon
A lantern containing a fox-tail curve.

## Wordmark

```text
EMBERFOX
Living Systems Cartographer
```

The wordmark should use sturdy, humanist lettering rather than ornamental fantasy script. The mark belongs in a living technical repository, not on the door of a suspiciously expensive potion shop.

## Accessibility

- Maintain at least 4.5:1 contrast for text uses.
- Do not rely on color alone to distinguish states.
- Preserve a clear silhouette at 32 px.
- Keep the compass diamond visible in monochrome.
- Provide descriptive alt text: `Fox head within an open lantern compass, flanked by feather-leaves beneath a star.`
