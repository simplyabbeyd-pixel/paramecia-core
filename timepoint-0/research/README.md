# Timepoint 0 Research Queue

This folder is for audits, comparisons, unresolved terminology, and future deep-research outputs. Research notes are not canon until promoted into `../canon/`.

## Immediate audit questions

- Is `Dervkin` the ancient ethnonym and `Deerkin` the later umbrella term, or is one a typo?
- Is `Dunehed` the intended early name for Sunscale Crosskin?
- Is `Swarmfolk` an internal name, archaic term, or outside label for Webdusk Crosskin?
- Are Witness-Path Kin a species, path-based people, process people, or a deliberately mixed category?
- Which crosskin develop physical adaptations later, and at what timepoint?
- Which shared practices alter architecture, food systems, language, ritual, and governance?
- How do friendship routes become movement routes without importing settlements too early?
- What ecological limits prevent every biome from becoming equally connected?

## Research standards

1. Separate canon, inference, and proposal.
2. Cite sources for real-world ecology, anthropology, settlement theory, and systems design.
3. Flag direct analogies that risk flattening living cultures.
4. Test every proposed addition against the Timepoint 0 locality constraint.
5. Prefer ecological causality over decorative complexity.
6. Preserve tensions and failure modes, not only harmonious outcomes.
