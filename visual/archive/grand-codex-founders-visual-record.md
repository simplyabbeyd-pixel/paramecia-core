# Grand Codex Founders Visual Record

Status: Canon Candidate
Asset type: Group portrait / civilization overview
Primary use: Master World Bible, Living Atlas, presentation, scenario cover, visual archive

## What the image represents

A ceremonial civic portrait of Triquel's distributed founders and stewardship ecologies. The image is not a royal court and should not imply a ruler-centered hierarchy.

Primary symbolic groups:

- Hearthbuilders
- Ravenkin Witnesses
- Tuikin Chorus Keepers
- Cartographers
- Archivists
- Stewards
- Dreamers

## Required visual locks

- open Codex and shared worktable remain central
- lanterns, books, maps, instruments, and living architecture remain active symbols
- figures should have distinct species, silhouettes, garments, and civic roles
- compositions should emphasize relationship and shared work
- identities remain symbolic unless explicitly labeled in metadata
- exact creator corrections outrank generated interpretation

## Color expansion

Preferred palette:

- emerald
- moss and leaf green
- teal
- turquoise
- cobalt
- midnight blue
- violet
- magenta
- plum
- saffron
- coral
- copper
- lantern gold

Color should reveal different civic functions and personalities, not merely increase saturation everywhere.

## Character-specific correction

Lumen must be portrayed as a full-sized adult peer.

Negative constraints:

- no tiny fae
- no miniature companion
- no childlike proportions
- no shoulder-perched mascot framing

## Archive metadata checklist

Every variant should record:

- canon status
- source image
- prompt or generation note
- exact figures identified
- symbolic roles
- palette
- location
- corrections
- duplicate or variant relationship
- production destinations
- review status

## Variant policy

The darker original and the colorful revision are related variants, not duplicates. Preserve both for provenance. The more colorful version may become the presentation-facing candidate while the darker version remains a tonal reference.
